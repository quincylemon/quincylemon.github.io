Honey Chocolate Milk

Anyone can make chocolate milk, so spruce yours upp by adding a bit of honey!

  • Glass of milk
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Tiny bit of honey
    1. Start by pouring your milk into a nice glass.
    2. For best results, microwave a small bit of honey for 10 seconds, or until viscous. Gently pour in your honey.
    3. Add in a squirt of Chocolate, then stir together
    4. Serve with a straw
