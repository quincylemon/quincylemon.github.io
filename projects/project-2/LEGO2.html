Step 1: Organize Your Pieces

Whether you organize by color or by part, its always a good idea to have system in place to help you find the part your need. If your strapped for time, just making sure every part is flat against the table with nothing underneath is a good strategy, though it takes a trained eye to see what you might need in the mix.
